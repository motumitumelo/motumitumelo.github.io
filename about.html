About Qatar Airways

Qatar Airways, the national carrier of the State of Qatar, is more than just an airline; we are your gateway to the world. Founded in 1993, we have grown into a multi-award-winning global leader in aviation, recognized for our unwavering commitment to excellence and innovation. Our extensive network connects you to over 170 destinations across six continents, seamlessly linking you to vibrant cultures, breathtaking landscapes, and unforgettable experiences.
