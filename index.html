About Qatar Airways: Soaring Above Expectations

Qatar Airways, the national carrier of the State of Qatar, is more than just an airline; we are your gateway to the world. Founded in 1993, we have grown into a multi-award-winning global leader in aviation, recognized for our unwavering commitment to excellence and innovation. Our extensive network connects you to over 170 destinations across six continents, seamlessly linking you to vibrant cultures, breathtaking landscapes, and unforgettable experiences. We take pride in our state-of-the-art fleet, featuring the latest in comfort and technology. Whether you're seeking luxurious Qsuites, spacious Economy seating, or exceptional Business Class amenities, we cater to every travel style and preference.

Extensive Network

Travel seamlessly with our vast network connecting over 170 destinations across six continents. We bring the world closer, opening doors to diverse cultures and experiences.

Award-Winning Fleet

Experience comfort and innovation aboard our state-of-the-art fleet. Choose from spacious Economy seating, luxurious Qsuites featuring private cabins, and premium Business Class amenities.

In-Flight Entertainment

Stay entertained throughout your journey with our Oryx One entertainment system. Enjoy a curated selection of movies, TV shows, music, and games. Access Wi-Fi on most flights to stay connected.

Onboard Cuisine

Indulge in our award-winning onboard dining experience. Savor delicious, globally-inspired dishes prepared with fresh, high-quality ingredients. We cater to diverse dietary needs and preferences.

Online Booking

Book your flights, manage your reservations, and access trip information easily through our user-friendly website and mobile app.

Luxurious Airport Lounges

Unwind and recharge in our exclusive airport lounges before your flight. Enjoy complimentary refreshments, Wi-Fi access, dedicated work areas, and comfortable seating.

Car Rentals

In collaboration with trusted car rental partners at your destination airport or city, you can seamlessly book a car alongside your flight, ensuring convenient transportation upon arrival.

Privilege Club

Join our Privilege Club, our exclusive loyalty program, and earn Qmiles with every flight. Redeem your Qmiles for future flights, upgrades, excess baggage allowance, and more.
